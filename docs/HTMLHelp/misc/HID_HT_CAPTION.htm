Title Bar

 

The title bar is located at the top of the main HexEdit window. It contains the name of the application and the name of the currently active file, if any. You can move the window by dragging the title bar.

 

The title bar also contains an HexEdit icon (at the leftt end) and buttons (at the right end):

 Click the icon to open the Control menu.  Double-click to close HexEdit.

 Click the minimize button to reduce the window to an icon.

 Click this to restore the window to its previous size and location

 Click the maximize button to enlarge the window to fill the screen (or current monitor on a multi-monitor system).

 Click this to close HexEdit . You are prompted to save changes.
